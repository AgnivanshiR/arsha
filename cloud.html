Services

Cloud & Infrastructure

  • Cloud Solution: Our Cloud Solutions provide secure, scalable, and reliable cloud services that meet the needs of businesses of all sizes. We offer a range of cloud deployment models, including public, private, and hybrid clouds, as well as cloud management services to ensure optimal performance.
  • End User Computing: Our End User Computing services provide secure access to your applications and data from any device, anywhere. We offer a range of solutions to meet your needs, from virtual desktop infrastructure to mobile device management.
  • Server Storage & Backup: Our Server Storage & Backup services provide reliable and cost-effective storage solutions for your critical data. We offer a range of storage options, from local storage to cloud-based storage, and our backup services ensure that your data is always protected.
  • Server Virtualization & Consolidation: Our Server Virtualization & Consolidation services help you maximize the value of your hardware investments by consolidating multiple servers onto a single hardware platform. We provide a range of virtualization solutions that help reduce costs, increase efficiency, and improve performance.
  • Network & Securities: Our Network & Security services provide comprehensive security solutions to protect your network and data from threats. We offer a range of security solutions, from firewalls and intrusion detection to security consulting and risk assessment services.
  • Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery: Our Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery services provide comprehensive disaster recovery planning and management solutions. We help you create a robust business continuity plan and provide ongoing management and support to ensure your business can continue operating in the event of a disaster.
  • Hosting & Data Center Services: Our Hosting & Data Center Services provide secure, reliable, and scalable hosting solutions for your applications and data. We offer a range of hosting options, from dedicated hosting to cloud-based hosting, as well as data center management services.
  • RIMS - Remote Infra Management Services: Our RIMS services provide remote management and support for your IT infrastructure. We offer 24/7 monitoring, proactive maintenance, and support services to ensure your IT infrastructure is always running smoothly.
  • Software & Licenses: Our Software & Licenses services provide access to the latest software and licensing options to meet your business needs. We offer a range of software solutions, from productivity software to enterprise applications, and can help you choose the right licensing model for your business.
  • Managed Services: Our Managed Services provide comprehensive management and support for your IT infrastructure. We offer a range of services, from help desk support to full IT outsourcing, and can customize our services to meet your specific needs.
  • Surveillance: Our Surveillance services provide you with comprehensive security solutions that protect your business and your employees, ensuring that your operations run smoothly.
  • Telecom Services: Our Telecom Services cater to your communication needs, ensuring that your business remains connected and operational.
  • IT Infra Resilience: Our IT Infra Resilience services help ensure your IT infrastructure is resilient and can withstand unexpected events. We offer a range of solutions, from business continuity planning to risk assessment, to help you prepare for the unexpected.
