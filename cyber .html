Services

Cyber Security

Security is a crucial aspect of any organization's digital presence. We offer a range of services to help you secure your systems and data.

  • Implementation of Security tools:
    Our team of cyber security experts can assist you with the implementation of security tools that are customized to your specific needs. We will ensure that your organization's systems are protected from cyber threats, and the data remains confidential.
  • ISO 27001, 27003 implementation & certification:
    We help our clients achieve ISO 27001, 27003 certifications to ensure compliance with international standards. Our team of experts will guide you through the implementation process and help you obtain certification, which demonstrates your commitment to information security management.
  • Data Protection:
    Data is the lifeblood of any organization, and it must be protected from external and internal threats. Our experts will help you implement data protection solutions, including data loss prevention (DLP), to safeguard your confidential data, and ensure data integrity.
  • Cyber Risk Governance implementation:
    We will help you to implement a robust cyber risk governance framework that will enable your organization to manage cyber threats, identify vulnerabilities and take necessary actions to minimize the risks. Our team will work with you to understand your business needs and create a customized risk management plan.
  • SOC (Security operation center services):
    We provide SOC services to our clients to monitor their systems and networks, detect threats, and respond to them in real-time. Our SOC team is equipped with advanced tools and techniques to monitor your systems 24/7 and provide you with proactive threat detection and remediation.
  • DCP DR:
    Our Disaster Recovery (DR) services are designed to ensure business continuity and minimize the impact of disruptive events. We help you to design and implement a robust Disaster Recovery Plan (DCP) that enables you to recover quickly in case of a disaster.
